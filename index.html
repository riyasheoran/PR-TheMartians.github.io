Instructions

  • Select the symbol by clicking on the respective symbol you want to choose.
  • Click in any box on the table shown in center of the screen.
  • Click on "PAUSE GAMESOUND" to pause the game sound.
  • Click on "PLAY GAMESOUND" to play the game sound.
  • Click on "RESTART GAME" if you wanna play again.
  • Reload the page if you want to start with another symbol.
